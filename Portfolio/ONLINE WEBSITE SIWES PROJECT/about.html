About LASU Online Learning

Learn more about our platform and how we're transforming education at Lagos State University.

Our Mission

The LASU Online Learning Platform is designed to provide students with flexible access to quality education, enabling them to learn at their own pace and convenience while maintaining the high academic standards of Lagos State University.

Our Vision

To become the leading e-learning platform in Nigerian Universities, providing innovative and accessible education to all LASU students regardless of their location or circumstances.

History

Established in 2020 during the global pandemic, the LASU Online Learning Platform was created to ensure continunity of education for all LASU students. Since then, it has grown to become an integral part of the university's teaching and learning ecosystem.
