Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the LASU Online Learning Platform.

How do i create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Register" button at the top right corner of the page. You'll need to provide your matriculation number, email address, and create a password. After registration, youll receive a confirmation email to activate your account.

What should I do if I forget my password?

If you've forgotten your password, click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and you'll recieve insructions on how to reset your password.

How do i enrol in a course?

After logging in, browse the available courses and click on the course you're interested in. On the course page, click the "Enroll" button. Some courses may require approval from your department.

Can I access course materials after the semester ends?

Yes, you can access course materials from previous semesters in your dashboard under "My Courses." However, some lecturers may choose to restrict access after a certain period.

What should I do if I can't access a course?

First, try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. If the problem persists, check your internet connection. If you stil can't access the course, contact our technical support team through the Help Center.

What browsers are supported?

The platform works best with the latest version of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend updating your browser to the latest version for optimal perfomance.

Are there any fees for using the platform?

The LASU Online Learning Platform is free for all registered LASU atudents. There are no additional fees for accessing course materials or submitting assignments.

Still have questions? Contact our support team for assistance.
